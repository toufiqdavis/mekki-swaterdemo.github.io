All You Need to Know About Us

mekki's water was founded in 1995 with a simple yet powerful vision - to provide people with the purest and most refreshing water. Our journey began with a small water purification plant, serving the local community with pristine drinking water.

As our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction grew, so did our operations. We expanded to serve neighboring towns and cities, always staying true to our core values of purity, health, and sustainability. Over the years, we have invested in advanced purification technologies and sustainable practices to ensure that every drop of mekki's water water meets the highest standards of excellence.
